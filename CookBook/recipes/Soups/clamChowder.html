Clam Chowder
An attempt to replicate Noah's Ark's Clam Chowder

Ingredients:

2 cans Cream of Potato
2 cans New England Clam Chowder
1 can Cream of Celery
2 cans Chicken of the Sea Minced Clams
1 stick butter
1 quart ½ and ½
1 onion, small
small amount of minced garlic

Directions:

  1. Sautee onion in butter.
  2. Put all in crockpot and let simmer, stirring occasionally.
