Cheese Ball
Ex Culina: Nancy Keser

Ingredients:

½ pound cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons sherry
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon mustard
½ teaspoon salt
a few grains of cayenne

Directions:

  1. Run the cheese through a food chopper.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the cheese.
  3. Mold mixture in a small bowl.

Comments: The ball may be rolled in chopped nuts.
