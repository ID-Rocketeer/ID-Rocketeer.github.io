Sweet and Sour Green Beans

Ingredients:

10 ounces fresh green beans, cut up or -
2 cups frozen green beans, cooked and drained
2 slices bacon, cut up
½ cup onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon flour
½ cup water
¼ cup vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon pepper

Directions:

  1. Place green beans in 1 inch water. (salted if desired)
  2. Heat to boiling, reduce heat.
  3. Boil uncovered for 5 minutes.
  4. Cover and boil 4 to 6 minutes drain.
  5. Cook bacon in 10" skillet, stirring occasionally, until crisp.
  6. Remove bacon.
  7. Drain fat from skillet and eserve 1 tablespoon.
  8. Cook onion in reserved bacon fat about 3 minutes stirring frequently.
  9. Stir in flour.
  10. Stir in remaining ingredients except beans.
  11. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
  12. Stir in beans.
  13. Cook until beans are heated through.
  14. Sprinkle with bacon.
