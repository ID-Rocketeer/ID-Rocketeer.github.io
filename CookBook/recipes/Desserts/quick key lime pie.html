Quick Key Lime Pie
Ex Culina: Camille Andreucci

Ingredients:

1 (6 oz.) ready-made graham cracker pie crust
14 ounces Magnolia Sweetened Condensed Milk
12 ounces Cool Whip
⅓-½ cup key lime juice - depending on how tart you like it (fresh is best but the bottled one works too. If you use fresh it takes about 8-10 key limes.)

Directions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl put 2 heaping dollups (use a ladle) of Cool Whip, pour in condensed milk & gently blend together with a whisk.
  2. Add key lime juice & blend well.
  3. Pour into graham cracker crust, cover and place in the refrigerator.
  4. Refrigerate for 24 hours.
  5. Just before serving spread a thick layer of remaining Cool Whip over the top.
  6. (Optional) Sprinkle crumbled graham crackers over the top.

Comments: If pie is not firm after 24 hours, place in freezer for about 10 minutes before adding topping. Lite Cool Whip may be used instead of regular. Chocolate graham cracker crust also works well.
