Pumpkin Pie
Grandma Messer's recipe for that Holiday favorite
Ex Culina: Velma Messer

Ingredients:

3 cups pumpkin
2 tablespoons heaping flour
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
½ teaspoon cloves
2½ cups milk
2 tablespoons honey
3 eggs

Directions:

  1. Mix ingredients together.
  2. Pour into 2 pie shells.
  3. Bake 350°.

Yield: 2 pies
