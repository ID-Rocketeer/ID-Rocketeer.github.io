Three Cheese Pasta Bake

Ingredients:

1 can Cheddar Cheese soup
¾ cup milk
¼ teaspoon pepper
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
6 cups cooked mostaccioli or ziti
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan Cheese
2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:

  1. In large bowl, combine soup, milk and pepper.
  2. Stir in mozzarella cheese.
  3. Add pasta, toss gently to coat.
  4. Spoon into 2 quart oblong baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over pasta mixture.
  6. In small bowl combine breadcrumbs and margarine.
  7. Sprinkle mixture in rows over cheese.
  8. Bake at 375° for 35 minutes or until hot and bubbling.
  9. Garnish with parsley and tomatoes if desired.
