Lasagna
A fairly simple but tasty recipe for Lasagna.
Ex Culina: The Internet

Ingredients:

1 pound ground beef
2 garlic cloves, minced
1½ cups water
15 ounces tomato sauce
6 ounces tomato paste
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 teaspoon dried oregano
½ teaspoon sugar
¼ teaspoon pepper
9 lasagna noodles
2 cups small curd cottage cheese
4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
2 cups grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. In a large saucepan, cook beef and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  2. Stir in water, tomato sauce and paste, soup mix, oregano, sugar and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Spoon ½ cup meat sauce into a greased 13×9×2 baking dish.
  5. Layer with three noodles and a third of the cottage cheese, mozzarella, meat sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Repeat layers twice.
  7. Cover and bake at 350° for 40 minutes or until bubbly and heated through.
  8. Uncover, bake 5-10 minutes longer.
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.
