Mince Ham Sandwich Filling
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 pound mince ham, ground
onion, chopped
pickle, chopped
2 eggs, hard boiled and mashed
salt
salad dressing, enough to moisten mixture

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Fill split weiner buns & sit with filling up on cookie tray.
  3. Warm in slow oven. (300Â°F)

Yield: 12 servings
