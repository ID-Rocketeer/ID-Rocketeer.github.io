Mostaccioli
The Mostaccioli Sauce recipe that originally appeared on the back of the R&F Mostaccioli package.
Ex Culina: R&F

Ingredients:

½ pound ground beef
½ pound ground pork
1 onion, chopped
3 slices bacon, chopped
¼ cup olive oil or salad oil
½ cup diced mushrooms
1 No. 2½ can Italian style peeled tomatoes (try Calf. plum style)
1 bay leaf
½ teaspoon basil leaf
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon black pepper
6 ounces tomato paste
1 tablespoon Sherry wine
1 pound mostaccioli
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Mix ground beef, pork and a few pieces of onion.
  2. Heat bacon in hot oil,
  3. Add meat, remaining onion, and diced mushrooms to bacon, stirring constantly, until well browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, bay leaf and basil.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper and simmer for 1 hour. If sauce becomes too thick, add water occasionally.
  6. Add tomato paste and Sherry wine and simmer another 15 minutes.
  7. Remove bay leaf.
  8. Pour sauce over cooked mostaccioli and top with grated Parmesan cheese.

Yield: 6-8 servings

Comments: Sauce is better prepared day before, cooked and kept in refrigerator.
