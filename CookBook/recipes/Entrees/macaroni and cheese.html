Macaroni and Cheese
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 cup macaroni (cooked)
¼ pound cheddar cheese (cubed)
salt & pepper
milk

Directions:

  1. Combine macaroni and cheese in a casserole dish.
  2. Season to taste with salt & pepper sprinkled on top.
  3. Pour in enough milk to almost cover the macaroni.
  4. Bake at 350° for about 40 minutes.
