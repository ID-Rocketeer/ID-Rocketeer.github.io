Spaghetti Sauce
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
salt & pepper
2 tablespoons flour
1 quart tomato juice
8 ounce tomato sauce
Italian seasoning

Directions:

  1. In a large quart sauce pan, brown hamburger with onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir in flour.
  3. Add tomato juice and tomato sauce and stir.
  4. Sprinkle on Italian seasoning until lightly covered, then stir in.
  5. Simmer for at least 1 hour.
