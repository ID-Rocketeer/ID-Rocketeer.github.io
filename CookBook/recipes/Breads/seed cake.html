Hobbit Seed Cake

Ingredients:

250 grams Plain Flour (8.818490487 oz, 2 cups)
½ teaspoon baking powder
1 pinch salt
150 grams soft butter (5.291094292 oz, 1.3227 sticks of butter)
150 grams caster sugar (5.291094292 oz, .6667 cups; .75 cups granulated sugar)
2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1½ tablespoons caraway seeds
3 large eggs
1½ tablespoons orange zest

Directions:

  1. Turn the oven on to 180C / 350F
  2. Grease and line with grease proof paper a 1LB loaf tin
  3. In a bowl sift together the flour, baking powder and salt
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, add the sugar and butter (soft) and cream together until they are a pale colour.
  5. Add in the eggs one at a time and add in the vanilla extract and orange zest ensuring it is mixed thoroughly
  6. Add in the dry ingredients a bit at a time and alternate adding in the milk until all the ingredients are added.
  7. Gently stir in the caraway seeds
  8. Using a spatula, spoon the mixture into the greased 1LB loaf tin and bake for 50 minutes or until cooked
  9. Once ready remove from the oven and leave in the tin for a further 10 minutes.
  10. Remove and place on a wire rack to cool
  11. Serve in slices and if desired spread with butter
