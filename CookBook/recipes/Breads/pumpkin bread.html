Pumpkin Bread
Ex Culina: Pat Powell

Ingredients:

3½ cups flour
2 teaspoons soda
1½ teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
2 cups sugar
1 cup oil
4 eggs
⅔ cup water
15 ounces canned pumpkin

Directions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients well.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and beat well.
  3. Bake in greased and floured bread pans for 45 minutes at 350°.

Yield: 2 loaves
