Chinese Beef and Peppers
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

3 pounds round steak, thinly sliced
6 tablespoons cornstarch
4 tablespoons oil
8 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons wine
1 dash Accent
salt & pepper
6 to 8 green peppers, cut in strips
6 tablespoons oil
4 slices chunk ginger
4 tablespoons sugar

Directions:

  1. Dredge beef in cornstarch; mix in soy sauce, oil, wine, Accent, salt & pepper.
  2. Saute green peppers in hot oil in Wok until slightly tender.
  3. Remove peppers; add ginger & beef.
  4. Stir fry until slightly brown.
  5. Add peppers & sugar and mix well.
  6. Serve over hot rice.

Yield: 8 servings
