Chicken in Mushroom Sauce
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

6 medium chicken breasts
½ cup dry white wine
1 can cream of mushroom soup
3 ounces canned sliced mushrooms (drained)
1 cup sour cream
hot cooked rice

Directions:

  1. Place breast skin side up in 12×7½×2 baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle with salt.
  3. Blend wine into soup, add mushrooms, and pour over chicken.
  4. Bake at 350° for 1 to 1¼ hours.
  5. Remove chicken to a platter.
  6. Pour sauce into saucepan, blend in sour cream, and heat gentle until hot.
  7. Serve sauce over chicken and rice.
