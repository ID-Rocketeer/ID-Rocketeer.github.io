Meatloaf
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1½ pounds ground beef
¾ cup oatmeal
¼ cup chopped onion
1½ teaspoon salt
¼ cup tomato juice
1 egg, beaten

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Pack firm into ungreased loaf pan.
  3. Bake at 350° for 1 hour 15 minutes.
  4. Let stand for 5 minutes before slicing.

Yield: 8 servings
