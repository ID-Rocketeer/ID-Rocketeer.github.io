Goulash
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
salt & pepper
2 cups macaroni
1 quart whole tomatoes

Directions:

  1. Brown hamburger with onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Start cooking the macaroni when the hamburger gets light brown.
  3. Combine tomatoes with the finished hamburger and macaroni.
  4. Cook over low heat until heated through (about 5 minutes).
