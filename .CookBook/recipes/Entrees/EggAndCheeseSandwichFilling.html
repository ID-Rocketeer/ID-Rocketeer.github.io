Egg & Cheese Sandwich Filling
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

8 eggs, hard boiled and mashed
½ pound Velveeta cheese, cut in small pieces
a little chopped onion for flavor
salt
salad dressing, enough to moisten mixture

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Fill split weiner buns & sit with filling up on cookie tray.
  3. Warm in slow oven. (300°F)

Yield: 12 servings
