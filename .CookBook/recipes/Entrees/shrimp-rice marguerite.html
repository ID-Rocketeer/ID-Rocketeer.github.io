Shrimp-Rice Marguerite
Shrimp, Rice, and Almonds in a Tomato based sauce
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

4 pounds large shrimp
3 tablespoons lemon juice
5 tablespoons salad oil
1 cup raw rice (cooked)
½ cup onion, minced
½ cup green pepper, minced
3 to 4 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon pepper
½ teaspoon mace
1 dash cayenne
2 cans condensed tomato soup
2 cups sour cream
2 cups slivered almonds (optional)
1 cups sherry
paprika

Directions:

  1. Marinate shrimp in lemon juice and oil for 2 hours; drain.
  2. Saute onion and green pepper in butter; add all ingredients except 1/4c almonds and paprika.
  3. Mix well; place in a 2 quart casserole.
  4. Sprinkle with remaining almonds and paprika.
  5. Bake at 350 for 55 minutes

Yield: 8+ servings

Comments: Note: To improve flavor, refrigerate for 12 hours or overnight before baking. The cook doesn't think she ever used more than about 2 pounds of tiny shrimp. The cook doubts she ever used even a cup of almonds. Use a pale dry sherry, not cooking sherry. An inexpensive brand will work fine. It is recommended by the cook that a larger baking dish be used to prevent overflow during cooking.
