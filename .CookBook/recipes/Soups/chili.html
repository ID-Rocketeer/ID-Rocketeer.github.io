Chili
Good for a cold winter night
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 pound Hamburger
1 onion (chopped)
1 Large Can Tomato Juice
1 Can Kidney Beans
1 Small Can Tomato Sauce
2 Tablespoon Flour
Salt
Pepper

Directions:

  1. Brown hamburger, Onion, Salt & Pepper
  2. Add Flour, Tomato Juice, Beans and Tomato Sauce
  3. Cover with Chili Powder
  4. Simmer 1 hour or more until proper consistency
