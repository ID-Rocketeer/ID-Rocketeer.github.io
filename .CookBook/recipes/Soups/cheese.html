Wisconsin Cheese Soup
Good for a cold winter night
Ex Culina: Sargento

Ingredients:

½ cup butter or margarine
½ cup all-purpose flour
5 cups milk
2 cans beer
4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
4 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
2 cups ham, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped

Directions:

  1. Melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in flour unitl smooth; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Stir in milk, beer, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, salt, cayenne, ham, and bell peppers.
  4. Heat to boil, stirring frequently.
  5. Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes, stiring frequently.
  6. Stir in cheese until melted.
