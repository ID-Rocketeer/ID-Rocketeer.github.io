Cranberry Bread
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

4 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon soda
1½ cups sugar
2 eggs
4 tablespoons melted butter
1 cup orange juice
4 tablespoons hot water
2 cups cranberries, cut in half
1 cup nuts

Directions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and mix.
  3. Bake for 1 hour at 350°.
  4. Remove from oven, immediately remove from pan, and wrap in foil.
  5. Place in refrigerator for 24 hours.

Yield: 1½ loaves
