Sage Dressing
Ex Culina: The Internet

Ingredients:

1 cup butter, melted
3 cups chopped onion
3 cups minced celery
16 slices bread, dried and broken into small pieces
6 tablespoons minced fresh sage or-
3 tablespoons dried sage leaves
1 cup minced fresh parsley
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
2 cups broth, or less

Directions:

  1. Put 4 tablespoons melted butter into skillet.
  2. Add onion and celery and cook, stirring often over moderate heat until vegetables are soft but not browned.
  3. In large bowl combine dried bread, sage, parsley, salt and pepper.
  4. Add onion and celery mixture and remaining melted butter.
  5. Toss until well mixed.
  6. Add broth slowly a little at a time while tossing mixture to moisten thoroughly.
  7. Take care to only add enough liquid to moisten or stuffing will be sodden.
  8. To test stuffing's seasoning, melt a little butter in a small skillet.
  9. Add rounded tablespoon of stuffing to skillet and stir until lightly golden.
  10. If it needs a more lively flavor add more sage, onion or celery.
