Beer
Ex Culina: Paul Messer

Ingredients:

6+ gallons water
malt
2 pound sugar
1 cake Fleischmann's Yeast

Directions:

  1. Boil 2 gallons of water, add malt and stir well.
  2. Boil for 25 minutes.
  3. Add sugar and stir well.
  4. Add enough water to bring level to 6 gallons.
  5. Let stand until luke warm.
  6. Add yeast, already disolved in water.
  7. Let stand for 2 days in a warm place, then bottle.

Comments: (optional) Add a washed and sliced potato to the brew.
