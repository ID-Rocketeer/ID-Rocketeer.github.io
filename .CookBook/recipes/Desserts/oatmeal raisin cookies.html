Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

½ cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
¼ cup milk
1⅔ cups oatmeal
1½ cups flour
1 teaspoon soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup raisins

Directions:

  1. Cream shortening and sugar, add eggs & milk.
  2. Combine with oatmeal and raisins.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Add flour, soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. Beat.
  6. Drop on greased cookie sheets and bake 12 minutes in a 350° oven.

Yield: 3 dozen cookies
