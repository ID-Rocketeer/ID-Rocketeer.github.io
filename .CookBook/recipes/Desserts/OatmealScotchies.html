Oatmeal Scotchies
Ex Culina: Nestlé Toll House Butterscotch Morsels

Ingredients:

1¼ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup butter or margrine, softened
¾ cups granulated sugar
¾ cups brown sugar, packed
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract, or
1 orange peel, grated
3 cups oatmeal, quick or old-fashioned
1⅔ cup butterscotch flavored morsels

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  3. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla extract in a large mixer bowl.
  4. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
  5. Stir in oats and morsels.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 7 to 8 minutes for chewy cookies or 9 to 10 minutes for crisp cookies.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Yield: 4 dozen cookies
