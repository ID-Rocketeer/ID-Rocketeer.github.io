Chocolate Cherry Cake
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 package Pillsbury Fudge Cake mix
1 can pie cherries
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon almond flavoring
1 cup sugar
5 tablespoons oleo
â…“ cup milk
6 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Mix cake mix, cherries, eggs, and almond flavoring in a large bowl with a spoon until well mixed.
  2. Pour into greased pan.
  3. Bake per cake mix instructions.
  4. Combine remaining ingredients in a pan.
  5. Boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  6. Pour over finished cake.
