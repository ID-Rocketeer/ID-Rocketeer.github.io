Old Fashioned Rice Pudding
Ex Culina: The Rival Company

Ingredients:

2½ cup cooked rice
1½ cup evaporated milk (or scalded milk)
⅔ cup brown or white sugar
3 tablespoons soft butter
½ teaspoon vanilla
½ to 1 teaspoons nutmeg
3 eggs, beaten
½ to 1 cups raisins

Directions:

  1. Thoroughly combine rice with all remaining ingredients.
  2. Pour into lightly greased Crock-Pot.
  3. Cook covered on high for 1-2 hours (low, 4 to 6 hours)
  4. Stir during 1st 30 minutes.
  5. Double recipe for 3½ or 4½ quart models.

Comments: Another version of this recipe was found on-line. It lists the butter as being 2 tablespoons and the vanilla as 2 teaspoons.
