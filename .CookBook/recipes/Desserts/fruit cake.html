Christmas Fruit Cake
Ex Culina: Grandma Brown (Matilda Catherine Addison Brown)

Ingredients:

1 pound ground pork fat
2 teaspoons baking soda
salt
pepper
2 cups boiling water
2 cups sugar
4 cups flour
2 teaspoons cinnamon
2 teaspoons allspice
2 teaspoons clove
1½ pounds raisins
1 pound candied fruit
1 cup nuts

Directions:

  1. Place salt, pepper, and soda on fat, pour the water over the fat, then break apart and let cool.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients.
  3. Bake in greased and floured pans at 350°.
