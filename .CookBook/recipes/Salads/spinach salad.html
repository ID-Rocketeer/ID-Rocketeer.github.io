Spinach Salad
A delicious use for Spinach
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 quart chopped spinach
¼ cup red onion (chopped)
½ cup celery (chopped)
1½ tablespoon wine vinegar
½ teaspoon tobasco sauce
½ teaspoon salt
3 eggs (hard boiled, chopped)
1 cup grated white cheddar cheese
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon horseradish

Directions:

  1. Wash spinach - dry well - cut.
  2. Mix spinach, onion, celery, eggs, cheese, & horseradish.
  3. Blend tabasco, salt, & vinegar.
  4. Add tobasco mix to spinach.
  5. Add mayonnaise to spinach immediately prior to serving.

Yield: 8-10 servings
