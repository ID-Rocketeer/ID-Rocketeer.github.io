Cranberry Salad
Grandma Collins' Thanksgiving and Christmas Cranberry Salad
Ex Culina: Barbara Collins

Ingredients:

1 pound cranberries
1 cup crushed pineapple
1 cup sugar
3 cups marshmallows
2 packages lemon jello
1 package dream whip

Directions:

  1. Grind cranberries, add pineapple, sugar and marshmallows - let stand.
  2. Make Jello using Â½ cup less water than package instructions call for.
  3. Add cranberry mixture to Jello and fold in Dream Whip.
